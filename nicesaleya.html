Nice "Cours Saleya"
Beautiful family apartment 50m from Sea

Stunning, bright, spacious and fully equipped holiday apartment in the centre of the old town of Nice, 50m from the world famous “Promenade des Anglais” and Cours Saleya. Completely renovated in 2005, it still retains the character of its original XVIIIth century builders. 50 meters from the sea, newly decorated in a modern contemporary style, the apartment has everything for the perfect family holiday. You will have every home comfort at your disposal, with independent air conditioning in all bedrooms + the lounge . If you travel with children we will do our best to make their stay comfortable and easy. You will be just 50 meters from the most beautiful beach in Nice, in the heart of the very picturesque old town in Nice (listed and protected area), 5 minutes to the central bus station and central shopping zone, 15 minutes to the central railway station, 10 minutes from Nice international airport by bus. This is the ideal location for people who want to get about without a car.

MORE ABOUT THE APPARTMENT

HOME

  • Very sunny, the apartment is full of charm with parquet floors, high ceilings, cornice, beautiful original wooden doors. Thick walls, independent air conditioning/heating system in all bedrooms & the lounge, and windows with louvre shutters keep it cool in summer, warm in the winter.
  • A polished wood parquet floor can be found in the lounge, while the bedrooms are tiled with “tomettes” from the XVIII (typical tiles hand-made in Salernes, Provence). All the furnishings in the apartment are brand new.
  • Suitability: Long-term Renters Welcome. Low Allergen Environment. Children welcome. Pets considered. Wheelchair inaccessible

DETAILS

  • TV set to watch video tapes and DVD. High speed internet connection.
  • The apartment is on the second floor above street level (semi-private entrance door). Its very secure. Stairs are easy to walk up.
  • Nearest Airport: 5 Kilometres
  • Nearest Barpub: 20 Metres (Lounge Bar in the building)
  • Nearest Ferry: 1 Kilometres
  • Nearest Train: 1 Kilometres
  • Nearest Motorway: 2 Kilometres
  • Nearest Beach: 50 Metres
  • Car: not necessary

MORE ABOUT NICE

  1. Whether you are a family with children wishing to enjoy relaxing vacations, or a group of night clubbers eager to have fun and entertainment, you will have a chance to enjoy the widest range of holiday options.
