With its large lounge/dining room, a modern kitchen, 3 bedrooms (one ensuite), and 2 bathrooms, the apartment comfortably sleeps from 6 to a max of 8, (with 2 people using the sofa bed in the lounge). MORE ABOUT THE APPARTMENT

HOME

  • Stunning, bright, spacious and fully equipped holiday apartment in the centre of the old town of Nice, 50m from the world famous “Promenade des Anglais” and Cours Saleya. Completely renovated in 2005, it still retains the character of its original XVIIIth century builders. 50 meters from the sea, newly decorated in a modern contemporary style, the apartment has everything for the perfect family holiday. You will have every home comfort at your disposal.
  • Very sunny, the apartment is full of charm with parquet floors, high ceilings, cornice, beautiful original wooden doors. A polished wood parquet floor can be found in the lounge, while the bedrooms are tiled with “tomettes” from the XVIII (typical tiles hand-made in Salernes, Provence). Thick walls, independant air conditioning/heating system in all bedrooms & the lounge, and windows with louvre shutters keep it cool in summer, warm in the winter.
  • It will be the perfect place for a great stay in Nice, whether for a family holiday or for a stay with friends. If you travel with young children we will do our best to make their stay comfortable and easy. We can provide a cot, high chair, and toys to make sure our youngest guests will keep a great memory of their holidays too.
  • We are happy to share our travel guides about the area with you, and when you arrive we shall send you the list of the restaurants we most like in Nice and its surroundings to make sure you enjoy your stay as much as possible. During your stay, we will be more than happy to talk about the area and help you in discovering the best spots or the most secret destinations.

MORE ABOUT NICE

  1. You will be just 50 meters from the most beautiful beach in Nice, in the heart of the very picturesque old town in Nice (listed and protected area), 1 minute from the tramway station (Opera Vieille Ville), 5 minutes to the central bus station and central shopping zone, 5 minutes to the central railway station, 15 minutes from Nice international airport by bus. This is the ideal location for people who want to get about without a car.
  2. Less than a minute walk away you will enjoy the daily fresh market on Cours Saleya, with its organic fresh food, fruits and vegetables, cheese, and bread. Restaurants and cafés around will offer lovely breaks around, after shopping, and on Mondays the Cours Saleya Flee market will provide a lovely change. A swim in the crystal clear water of the Baie des Anges can be experienced several times a day, as the flat is only 50meters away from the beach, unless you would rather go for an afternoon walk along “La Promenade des Anglais, 6 kms long, where cycling and rolling are also possible. With its lovely little shops for clothes, jewellery, home decoration and much more, the Old Town will be a magic place for shopping addicts, while the Matisse, Chagall or Contemporary art Museums will represent a very enjoyable cultural alternative option.
  3. To travel around, Nice is an ideal base from which to explore the Cote d'Azur: you can take a boat trip from Nice port to the Lerins islands, St. Tropez, la Corniche d'Or and Monaco. Monaco is 15 mins by train and Cannes is about 40 mins (the bus fare between two cities in the area costs only one euro).
